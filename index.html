The view of Northloop at night. It is bustling with restaurants, coffee shops, and bars

In Northloop, you will see many old buildings got revamped into modern hotels, restaurants, and apartments. It gives the "yuppy" vibes.

Targel Field at dusk

The home of the Twins, the baseball team that barely ever makes it to the Play-off. I am told that it is a curse for all Minnesotan teams, they are mediocre at best.

Hennepin Ave Bridge

The bridge and the sunset are just greate combination. Hennepin Ave bridge connects downtown Minneapolis and Northeast Minneapolis.

Under the bridge

The reflection of the Hennepin Ave bridge on the Mississipi River might be one of the things that you don't want to miss. The Grain Belt Beer Wall Art lights up one of the river bank, It is quite a view.

Downtown Minneapolis from St.Anthony Main

St. Anthony Main is home to restaurants, bars, art studios just a bridge away from Downtown Minneapolis. Integer Strolling down the street in Summer is a must.

Fall is probably my least favorite season

The trees drop all the leaves and it can look so eery. It seems like a resting time, but the world revolves all season.

Stone Arch Bridge in Winter

Another sport you have to visit when you come to Minneapolis. The bridge is gorgeous and the view is awesome.

3rd Ave bridge is under constructions

Mississipi River and its bridges. The reflection is just uncanny.

I enjoy the rain

We get a lot of rain in the Spring. It is the best part of the experience, when trees and flowees come back.

It looks like a wave

If you are lucky, you might see something like this. The clouds looked like about to eat the city.

I love the cloud

The combination of sunset, cloud, and the Twins Stadium will give you some chills and realize that we are just small part of the universe. The Sun might be set in Minneapolis, but it raises on the other side of the world.

Sunset from the rooftop

If you stay in a hotel with a rooftop, don't forget to enjoy the sunset. You won't regret it and you will get a lot of Instagramable pictures.

I love taking pictures and telling stories

I believe that pictures can deliever stories without saying anything. A sentence will go far when I put picture next to it. This collections of pictures will give viewers some idead about the city and its seasons. Minneapolis is small city, but it has its charm. It is growing and I love it.
